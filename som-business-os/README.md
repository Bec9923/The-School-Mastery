# School of Mastery Hub plugin

The School of Mastery Business OS plugin for Claude Co-Work. It connects to your
School of Mastery portal and runs your weekly rhythm with Bec's framework.

Everything the plugin does requires your personal **access code** (from your
portal). Without a code it does nothing, and it carries no framework content or
secrets, those live securely on the server.

Install from the marketplace, then run `/som-connect` and paste your access code.
